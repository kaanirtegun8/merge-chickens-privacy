🐣 Egg Factory : Farm Idle • Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

Egg Factory : Farm Idle is designed as a simple single-player mobile game. We believe privacy should be clear, transparent, and easy to understand. This page explains how information is handled when you use the app.

ℹ️ Overview

Egg Factory : Farm Idle (“the App”) is a single-player mobile game developed to provide a fun and lightweight gameplay experience. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is stored.

🔒 Information Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information through Egg Factory : Farm Idle.

The App does not require:

  • Account registration
  • Login credentials
  • Email address submission
  • Name, phone number, or personal profile details

No personal data is collected during gameplay.

📱 Local Storage

All gameplay-related data, such as game progress, gold, chickens, and settings, is stored locally on your device only.

This data:

  • Is not uploaded to any server
  • Is not accessible by the developer
  • Remains entirely on your own device

If you delete the App, locally stored game data may also be removed depending on your device settings.

🧩 Third-Party Services

Egg Factory : Farm Idle does not use third-party services for:

  • Analytics
  • Advertising
  • Tracking
  • User profiling

The App operates without external data-sharing tools or integrations.

👶 Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from users of any age, including children.

Because no personal information is collected, Egg Factory : Farm Idle is designed to be privacy-friendly for all players.

📝 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any future changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last Updated” date at the top will be revised accordingly.
